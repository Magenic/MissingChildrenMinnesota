</td></tr>
<tr><td>&nbsp</td></tr>
<tr>
<td>
Missing Children Minnesota is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, 
and the oldest in the Midwest dedicated to the safe return of missing 
children and child personal safety education.
</td>
</tr>
<tr><td>&nbsp</td></tr>
<tr>
<td>
We are here to help families find the missing children. We are here 
to provide child safety education and abduction prevention programs 
parents, children, and people who work with children.
</td>
</tr>
<tr><td>&nbsp</td></tr>
<tr>
<td>
Some of our staff and board members have been personally affected by 
the abduction or disappearance of a child. All are concerned citizens 
who dedicate time, talent and hard work on behalf of the safety, 
education and well-being of children.
</td>
</tr>
<tr><td>&nbsp</td></tr>
<tr>
